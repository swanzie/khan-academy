Travel to Taiwan

Taiwan is an island off the eastern coast of China. The capital is Taipei. There are many things to eat and do in Taiwan, so it's a great place to visit!
Some of the cities in Taiwan are:

  • Taipei
  • Tainan
  • Taoyuan
  • Chiayi

Things to do

  • Taipei 101 was built in 2004, it was the tallest building in the world. In 2010, another it was surpassed, but it is still impressive and has shops and restaurnts inside.
  • Night market - Check out the night markets in Taiwan where you can eat a variety of street foods and do lots of shopping.
  • National Palace Museum captures much of China's history and includes many artifacts that were saved during the cultural revolution.
  • Alishan Mountain - Alishan is a mountain range in the central-southern region of Taiwan. The highest peak in the mountain is 8,737 ft.
